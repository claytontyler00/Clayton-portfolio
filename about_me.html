About Me

Thank you for checking out my portfolio. I'm currently working on gaining experience in UX Design at Mycase. I am currently mentoring for a UX role and am working on game changing experiences for Lawyers and their clients. My role as a designer in the world of Legal practice involves looking to see how Cognitive technology can analyze the vast trove of legal data available today at an incredible speed, and I design systems that deliver these deep, actionable insights to legal professionals in a way that enhances their existing workflows. I also conduct generative user research with users to gain awareness into their pain points and user needs, and perform usability testing to optimize our products.

I live in Santa Barbara with my Partner Christian and our dog Zuko. We love taking our dog to the beach and going on hikes in the mountains.
