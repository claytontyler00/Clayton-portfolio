About Me

Hi, I'm Clayton and I'm a User Experience Designer. I'm currently gaining experience in UX Design at Mycase while creating game changing experiences for Lawyers and their clients. I conduct generative user research to gain awareness into their pain points, needs, and perform usability testing to optimize our products.

I specialize in Users. From interfaces to experiences my goal is to create things that are intuitive. I Design vector based solutions for the Web & Product and even developed this website using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and a touch of Googling, See for yourself.

I've enjoyed spending the last 5 years in Santa Barbara with my partner Christian and our dog Zuko. I recently graduated from UCSB and quickly started my career at Mycase. Along the way I found a passion for interaction design and research, and I use those passions to create interfaces that solve problems. When I'm not creating beautiful solutions, I enjoy reading books, playing videogames, and loving my beautiful family.
